1.Description:
This mod is a simple compatibility patch for Glass Glowset by Solidfire and Better Morrowind 
Armor by Moranar.
=================================================================================================
2.Installation:
Simply drop the "meshes" folder in your "data files" folder and let the meshes overwrite.
Be sure to install after both Glass Glowset and Better Morrowind Armor

=================================================================================================
3.Conflicts:
None

=================================================================================================
4.Requirements:
Better Bodies
Better Morrowind Armor by Moranar
Glass Glowset by Solidfire
